Black Beetle is a landscape architectural practice focused on providing thoughtful and innovative design solutions that respond to our clients’ brief, based on our understanding of the social, environmental and functional concerns affecting today’s landscape.

The two Directors, Giselle Barron and Ilia Kokalevski are Registered Landscape Architects and are experienced in the design and documentation of a wide variety of projects throughout Australia in a variety of environments and context, from government, multi residential, schools and institutions, commercial projects, public plazas, private residential and site planning.

From our Sydney office Black Beetle’s creative team has local and international experience designing various Commercial, Residential and Environmental landscapes. Our designs focus on melding nature and architecture to create quality designs that are inspirational, fresh, cost effective and practical for many years to come.

We look forward to working closely in conjunction with you on this project to achieve the best possible design solution.
